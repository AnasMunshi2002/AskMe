Who we are

Empowering the world to develop technology
through collective knowledge.

AM techstart is a crowdsourced Question and answer website that connects people with experts in various fields. Our mission is to provide a platform for people to ask questions and get answers from experts in their respective fields.

We believe that everyone should have access to expert knowledge and advice, regardless of their background or financial situation. Our platform is designed to be easy to use and accessible to everyone.

Whether you're a student looking for help with homework, a professional seeking advice on a project, or just someone who's curious about the world around them, AM techstart has something for you.

Our public platform serves 100 million people every month, making it one of the most popular websites in the world.

Our asynchronous knowledge management and collaboration offering, AM TechStart for Teams, is transforming how people work.
